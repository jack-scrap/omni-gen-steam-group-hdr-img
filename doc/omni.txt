# Vehicles
## Crane
### Navigation
`zoom(d)` - Move forward, back
`pan(d)` - Translate head left and right
`ped(d)` - Translate head up and down

`grab()` - Grab container

## Truck
This allows for complete freedom of movement without being restricted to an axis.

`zoom(d)` - Move forward, back

`left(d)` - Turn left
`right(d)` - Turn right
